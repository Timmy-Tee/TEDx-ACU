Our Speakers

A little about our Speakers


Adebola Zoe Williams

Brand Storyteller

Adebola Williams is the top Brand Storyteller in Africa. She’s currently the Brand Storyteller at Moni, a fintech startup creating shared prosperity in Nigeria, Benin Republic, and Guinea, Conakry. Asides working in tech, she helps creatives like her transition to working in tech and is also building a community of storytellers who will impact the way marketing is being done all over the world.
She strongly believes in a world where creatives contribute to the growth and profiting of tech companies and she’s helping to build such world with her writing, mentoring, teaching sessions. Her goal is to help every creative access the part of them that grows brand equity and company revenue.
Her favourite thing to do is collaborate and partner with brands that take impact seriously. You can find her on Instagram laughing loudly, on Twitter dishing out tech marketing gems, on LinkedIn being a human being, on Medium baring her soul out and on TikTok figuring out how it works.

Tomiwa Ikuejuyone

Business and Brand Strategist

Babtomiwa Ikuejuyone is the Group Head, Corporate Communications and Special Projects at Investment One Financial Services Limited (formerly GTB Asset Management). Tomiwa has over 10 years’ experience working in banking, financial services and investment management. Tomiwa holds a degree in Economics from Ajayi Crowther University Oyo. He is certified ISO Lead Implementer and has various professional certificates both locally and internationally.
He Started his career at Credit Direct Limited (Subsidiary of FCMB Group) with the Internal Control, Compliance and Governance unit where he started as a branch internal control officer and rose to State and later Regional Control Officer South South-East. During his stay at Credit Direct he headed so many sub committees that gave to so many initiatives with his team and organization at large.
He Joined Investment One as Head of Strategy and New Business and was able to transform Investment One into a global brand and was subsequently promoted to Group Head, Corporate Communications and Special Projects. His areas of expertise are Strategy, Brand Communications, Project management, Internal Control & Audit, Investment Management and International Business. Tomiwa has a passion for helping young professionals and startups; he just started a regular mentoring program for young professionals steering them in the right direction of their chosen careers and helping them grow into future industry leaders. He is a strong believer in the future of Africa and the potential in African youths. He believes that tenacity (Not giving up), adaptability (Ability to adapt to changing environmental policies) and foresight (Long term strategic vision/planning) are critical traits to succeed in business in Africa.

Timothy Ayodele

CEO, FireSwitch Technologies Ltd
&
Sheda House.

Ayodele Timothy Olusegun acquired coding skills while training as an electrical electronic engineer at Igbinedion University, Okada. As an astute believer in creating solutions, he founded MoreTims Ltd during NYSC in 2016 which metamorphosed into a renewable energy firm in 2018. He founded Fire-Switch Technologies Ltd in 2019. A company, whose primary business is innovating solutions to challenges through exploring tech tools, leveraging on artificial intelligence and blockchain methods of implementation. He co-founded Sheda House Ltd in 2020. An organization whose primary business is branding, marketing and skill acquisition. Using unique methods, and leveraging on a mentorship system has trained hundreds of young people to become software developers, designers’ photographers, managers and so on. He has over the years ideated and created products in fintech, commerce, accounting, health, many of which are live and some of which will launch in months ahead. He believes in creative ideas and a lover of God.

Inemesit Hanson

Writing Consultant & Publisher

Inemesit Hanson is the founder of Your Writing Camp, a school for writers where she helps writers write, publish and market great books as well as teach writers the business of writing. Via her brand, Your Writing Camp she has published books for celebrities and Influencers. One of such books include Filters, the book of BBNaija Reality TV Star, Liquorose. She is qualified to serve as a publisher because she is a graduate of Library and Information Science. She has also worked with over 30 brands (skin care brands, food brands, makeover brands) who want to leverage writing to increase sales and visibility. From time to time she coaches writers 1 on 1 on monetization tactics to earn from writing and learn the nitty gritty of writing skills they hope to explore. She is the convener of the Profitable Writers Conference,a free conference that aims at eradicating poverty amongst writers and creatives in general. She has convened these free conferences for the past two years across Lagos state, Abuja (FCT) and Uyo, Akwa Ibom state. She hopes to expand to more states in time to come. Inemesit is a five time author with her latest book being "It's not just Writing." She also has many writing short courses and is a digital creator. She was the 2020 Programs Officer for the YALI Network Abuja, An Initiative of the US Embassy. She is has also graced many stages as a public speaker.

Babajide Ojajune

Transformational Coach &
Creativity Expert

A dynamic public speaker and writer, a mentor, a Counsellor, an advocate of light, a transformational coach, a creativity expert, a behaviorist psychologist and therapist. He is passionate about helping men to activate the right mindset that is needed to live to their full potential and guiding them to unleash the creativity that fuels them into becoming the better versions of themselves.

He has worked on projects such as “Instruction for the Tetiary” (IFT) that is aimed at getting student ready for real life events by making life changing decisions that guides them in the next transition season of their lives. He is also the creator of Gateway Mentoring institute (GMI) and Cave adulam transformational and training Academy (CATTA) Both aimed at building and guiding young people into developing the mindsets that unlocks Greatness and opens them up to the pathway of self-evolution.
